Final Exam Requirement

The primary procedural milestone for the M.S. thesis degree is the final exam, i.e., the defense of the thesis, which typically happens in the fourth semester of graduate study. The final exam requirement for the M.S. thesis option requires that the student orally defend a written thesis before a suitably constituted advisory committee (see Section 9).
