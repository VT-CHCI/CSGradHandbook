PhD Exam Requirements

The final exam requirement for the Ph.D. degree requires that the degree candidate make an oral presentation of his or her research work and defends the significance and accuracy of this work in response to questions from the student's advisory committee. The committee, in closed session, determines whether the student has successfully completed the examination. This exam can be attempted at most twice.
